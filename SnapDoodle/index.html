Get Started

Snap Doodle is a digital typeface that goes beyond alphabets.
The grid below is a canvas to create most anything.

Check out the instructions of how to use the grid and the sample
typeface below before you begin your own creative journey.

The only constraint is the grid form!

1. Hover on the grid to see a possible selection in blue.

2. Click on the segment you want to display.

3. Do not worry about error. Hover and click again to erase.

4. Edit and create anything that comes to mind! (reload page to clear canvas)
